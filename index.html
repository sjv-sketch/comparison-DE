# Famly DE Comparison Page — Prototype Guide **Status:** Concept prototype — content is placeholder, functionality is final **Purpose:** Align on structure and logic before writing a developer spec --- ## What this prototype shows This is an interactive HTML prototype built to demonstrate how the comparison page works — not to show final copy. The content (feature rows, quotes, FAQ answers) is placeholder and will be replaced with validated content from the sales team before development begins. The core idea is that one page does the work of many: instead of building separate static pages for every competitor and every persona, a single page adapts to who is reading it and what system they're coming from. --- ## How it works ### Two selectors at the top Visitors (or sales reps) choose two things: 1. **Who they are** — Kita-Leitung, Träger / GF, Erzieher:in, or Fachberatung 2. **What system they use today** — KiKom, Kitalino, Kitaplus, Tabellen & E-Mails, or Alle Systeme (generic) Every time either selector changes, the entire page updates: the comparison table, the section headers, the metrics strip, the customer quote, and the FAQ answers all reflect that specific combination. ### What changes per selection **Competitor selector** changes: - The metrics strip at the top (e.g. "99.9% uptime" is relevant against KiKom's stability issues, "0€ DSGVO risk" is relevant against Tabellen) - The table column header and competitor name throughout - The customer quote (a different quote for each competitor) - The FAQ objections and answers **Persona selector** changes: - The table sections and rows (a Träger sees "Echtzeit-Dashboard alle Standorte" as the first row; an Erzieher:in sees "Anzahl Logins täglich") - The section labels (e.g. "Was Sie täglich kostet" for Kita-Leitung vs. "Träger-Übersicht & Kontrolle" for Träger) - The customer quote (same competitor, different persona perspective) - The FAQ questions and answers (persona-specific objections) The context banner just below the selectors always confirms the current view: *"Sie sehen den Vergleich als Kita-Leitung — Famly vs. KiKom"* --- ## Two use cases, two audiences ### 1. Generic version — public-facing The **Alle Systeme** option is the version we publish on famly.co. It compares Famly against a generic "Ihr aktuelles System" without naming any competitor. This is safe to publish, good for SEO, and works for any visitor who lands on the page organically. The persona selector still works here — someone who identifies as a Träger sees different rows than an Erzieher:in, even in the generic version. ### 2. Competitor-specific versions — sales tool The KiKom, Kitalino, Kitaplus, and Tabellen versions are more direct. They name specific weaknesses, reference post-acquisition risks, and use pointed language that would be inappropriate on a public page. These versions are intended as a sales enablement tool — sent by SDRs or AEs as a link during or after a prospect conversation. The sales rep knows which competitor the prospect uses and can say: *"I'm sending you a comparison — it's specifically built for teams coming from KiKom."* The page doesn't say "KiKom" in the URL, just in the content — so it's shareable without being aggressively public. --- ## What is placeholder vs. final Everything marked `[PLACEHOLDER — Alex liefert Inhalt]` needs to be replaced before development.
